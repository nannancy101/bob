My Netflix Recommendations

Summary: The story follows Violet Evergarden's journey of reintegrating back into society after the war is over and her search for her life's purpose now that she is no longer a soldier. In the army, she was treated as a weapon or war tool rather than a human being. This caused to her be indifferent to other people's feelings and emotions and the only person who treated her like an actual human being was her commanding officer, Gilbert. However, during the last battle of the war, Gilbert "sacrificed" his life to save her from being blown up. His last words her to was "I love you". Violet, being the indifferent and emotionless, is incapable of understanding the meaning behind his words. Crazy ikr. After the war, she goes on a journey to find out the meaning behind those words. She encounters a job as a Memory Doll, a person that writes letters for others, and discovers all the different emotions in the world. She is no longer an emotionless doll, but an amazing and emotional person.

Review: This is one of my favorite series on Netflix. Everything about this show is perfect from the music to the animations to the story plot. It brings the audience on an emotional rollarcoaster making you feel empathy and hatred towards some of the characters. I can't tell you how many times I cried watching all 13 episodes. The soundtrack is so amazing, I played it loop for two weeks straight.
